# Progression Rule

For every exercise:

Example (8-12 reps)

Week 1:

* 10, 9, 8

Week 2:

* 11, 10, 9

Week 3:

* 12, 11, 10

Week 4:

* 12, 12, 12

Increase weight and restart near the bottom of the range.

# One Last Important Thing

No, **all exercises should not be 6-12 reps.**

A better rule is:

### Heavy Compound Lifts

* 6-10 reps
* Incline Press
* Chest Press
* Hack Squat
* Rows
* Pulldowns
* Shoulder Press

### Isolation Exercises

* 8-15 reps
* Curls
* Pushdowns
* Extensions
* Pec Deck

### Small Muscles

* 12-20 reps
* Lateral Raises
* Rear Delt Fly
* Wrist Curls

This gives excellent hypertrophy while keeping joints happier and fatigue lower than forcing every exercise into the same 6-12 rep range.
